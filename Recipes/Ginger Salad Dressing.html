Ginger Salad Dressing

★★★★

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Difficulty: Medium Servings: 1.75 Source: Food.com

Ingredients

1/2 cup minced onion

1/2 cup avocado oil

1/3 cup rice vinegar

2 tbsp water

2 tbsp minced fresh ginger

2 tbsp minced celery

2 tbsp ketchup

4 tsp soy sauce

2 tsp sugar

2 tsp lemon juice

1/2 tsp minced garlic

1/2 tsp salt

1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper

Directions

1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.

2. Blend on high speed for about 30 seconds or until all of the ginger is well-pureed.

3. This recipe yields 1 3/4 cups.

Notes

The original recipe was a little bit heavy on the soy sauce flavor
