Jabalaya

★★★★

Difficulty: Medium Source: Dad

Ingredients

2 pounds chicken parts

1/2 tsp cayenne

2 tbsp oil

8 ounces andouille sausage

1 onion

1 bell pepper

1 celery stalk

1 tsp dried thyme

2 cloves garlic

1 cup rice

1 can tomatoes

1 cup fish stock (or clam juice)

1 cup chicken stock

1 bay leaf

1/2 pound shrimp

4 green onions

1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce

Directions

Pat chicken dry and sprinkle with cayenne
Heat oil in large sauce pan to medium high
Cook chicken for 4 minutes on each side
Add sausage and stir for 3 minutes until it browns
Remove chicken and sausage
Add onion, bell pepper, celery and garlic to skillet and cook
Stir frequently until vegetables begin to soften
Add the rice and thyme and stir to combine
Add tomatoes and break up with masher or spoon
Stir in fish stock, chicken stock and bay leaf
Return chicken and sausage to pot
Bring mixture to a simmer
Reduce heat, cover and cook for 15 minutes
Distribute the shrimp over the top, pressing them into the rice
Cook covered for 15 more minutes until the chicken and shrimp are cooked and the rice is tender
Stir in the chopped green onions and hot sauce to taste
