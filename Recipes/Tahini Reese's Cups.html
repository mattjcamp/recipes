Tahini Reese's Cups

★★★

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Total Time: 25 minutes Difficulty: Medium Servings: 9

Ingredients

1/2 cup tahini

1/2 cup powdered sugar

8 oz chocolate

2-3 tsp cold water

1 tbsp Flaky salt

Directions

Melt the chocolate in a Pyrex in the microwave, in 30-second bursts, stirring until smooth and glossy and combined.

In a separate bowl, combine water with tahini one teaspoon at a time until seized and thick.

Add the powered sugar and mix To Make Traditional Cups

Set out 9 regular size cupcake liners. pour a small puddle in each of the liners, and swirl around to coat the bottom.

Plop a teaspoon of tahini in each, squishing it down with your finger to lay it flat.

Pour remaining chocolate evenly over the tops of the tahini. It's okay to eyeball this

To Make Easier Sheets

Line a small baking sheet with parchment paper

Spread a layer of chocolate on the paper

Put in freezer for 15 minutes

Spread the Tahini mixture onto the chocolate 11. Put in freezer for 15 minutes

Spread the remaining chocolate on the list 13. Put into freezer

Finish Up

Finish with flaky salt.

Keep in the fridge until you're ready to eat
