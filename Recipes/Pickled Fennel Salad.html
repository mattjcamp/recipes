Pickled Fennel Salad

★★★

Prep Time: 10 Cook Time: 5 Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2 Source: Blue Apron

Ingredients

1 Fennel Bulb

1 Golden Beet

1 Red Onion

1 Navel Orange

2 Tablespoons Red Wine Vinegar

2 Tablespoons Sugar

Directions

PREPARE

Wash and dry the fresh produce.
Peel and thinly slice the onion.
Peel the beet and cut it in half lengthwise; very thinly slice the halves.
Cut off and discard any fennel stems and fronds (the green, thread-like tops of the plant).
Halve the fennel bulb lengthwise; remove and discard the core.
Thinly slice the fennel bulb.
Cut off and discard peel and pith of the orange; medium dice the orange.

PICKLE

In a medium pan (nonstick, if you have one), combine the sugar, vinegar, onion and ¼ cup of water;
season with salt and pepper.
Heat to boiling on medium-high. Once boiling, cook 1 to 2 minutes, or until the liquid is slightly reduced in volume. Transfer the onions and pickling liquid to a heatproof bowl and set aside. Carefully rinse and dry the pan.

MAKE SALAD

Reserving 1 tablespoon of the pickling liquid, drain the pickled onions.
In a large bowl, combine the orange, fennel, beet and drained pickled onion.
Add the reserved pickling liquid; drizzle with olive oil and toss to coat.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
