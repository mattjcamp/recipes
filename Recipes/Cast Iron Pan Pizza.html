Cast Iron Pan Pizza

★★★★★

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 2 hrs Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: My Collection

Ingredients

72% Hydration Dough


150 grams (1 cup/150 grams) unbleached flour

3 grams (1 tsp/3 grams) yeast

1.5 grams (1/2 tsp/1.5 grams) coarse kosher

Pinch sugar

8 grams (1 tbsp/14 grams) olive oil

100 grams (1/2 cup/90 grams) water






Directions


1. Combine everything in a bowl and mix

2. Put mixture into food processor

3. Pulse 45 times

4. Remove dough and shape into a ball

5. Put the dough in a bowl and cover

6. Let rise for 2 hours or in the fridge for up to a week

7. Shape into a disk and add toppings in a cash iron or carbon steel pan

8. Let rest for about 20 minutes until it puffs slightly

9. Pre-heat the oven to 550°F

10. Cook for 14 minutes until cheese melted well and crust looks crisp

11. Let sit 5 minutes
