General Tso Chicken

★★★★

Difficulty: Hard Source: Joshuaweissman.com

Ingredients

CHICKEN MARINADE


1.5 lb. (700g) boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into pieces

1 tbsp (14g) Shaoxing wine

1 tbsp (14g) soy sauce

2 cloves garlic, mined

1 inch knob ginger, minced


SAUCE

1/2 cup chicken stock (120ml)

3 Tbsp (50g) soy sauce

3 Tbsp (45g) granulated sugar

1 tsp (4g) Chinese chili flakes


CHICKEN COATING


1/2 cup (75g) all purpose flour

1/2 cup (60g) cornstarch

oil for frying

pinch of salt


STIRFRY


Slurry

1.5 Tbsp (21g) vinegar

1 Tbsp (15g) corn starch


4 cloves garlic, minced

2 inch knob ginger, minced

5 dried red chili peppers (chilies de arbol, korean chilies, etc.)


1 Tbsp (14g) Shaoxing wine


GARNISHES

toasted sesame seeds for garnish

thinly sliced green onion for garnish

Directions

Prepare Chicken
- Start by cutting the chicken into bite-size pieces
- Place in a large bowl
- Add in grated ginger and garlic, Shaoxing wine, soy sauce, and toss together to combine
- Cover with plastic wrap and marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight in the fridge

Prepare Sauce
- add the Chinese chili flakes, sugar, chicken stock, soy sauce, msg, and stir together

Fry Chicken
- In a heavy bottom 5 quart pot, add in enough oil for frying (2.5” deep) and bring to 375F
- While the oil heats, coat the chicken by combining all-purpose flour and cornstarch in a medium-size bowl
- Whisk to combine
- Add a generous amount of chicken into the flour and toss until thoroughly coated
- Shake off the excess flour and place on a rimmed baking sheet before repeating with the rest.
- Carefully lower the chicken into the frying oil in batches and fry for 3-4 minutes
- Toss around to ensure no sticking.
- Once golden brown, remove from the oil and place on a wire rack in a rimmed baking sheet to cool
- Season with salt

Cook Sauce
- Make a slurry: in a small bowl whisk together cornstarch and vinegar until a slurry forms. Set aside.
- Heat a wok over medium-high heat then add in enough vegetable oil to coat and until shimmering
- Add in chili peppers, minced ginger and garlic along with the dried red chili peppers
- Cook and vigorously stir until the garlic just barely begins to toast
- Once garlic begins to toast, add in the Shaoxing wine and let it boil and cookout for 20 seconds
- Add the prepared sauce
- Let it come up to boil then taste for seasoning with salt.

- After boiling for 30 seconds, stream and stir in the cornstarch slurry from earlier
- Once thicken immediately remove from heat
- Add chicken and toss vigorously to coat.

- Plate the chicken and top with sesame seeds and finely sliced green onions
