Egg Rolls Mushik Style

★★★★

Difficulty: Medium Servings: 8 servings Source: Mom

Ingredients

18 eggs

6 shredded carrots

8 chopped scallions

7 minced cloves of garlic

3 celery stalks chopped

1/4 cup vegetable oil

1/4 cup soy sauce

egg roll dough

crisco

Directions

Sauté vegetables until tender in oil.

Beat eggs and then cook separately in oil until they make an omelet.

Cut omelet in pieces.

Mix cooked vegetables and eggs in bowl.

Add soy sauce.

Put 2 tbsp of mixture in egg roll dough.

Fold all sides.

Cook in deep oil or crisco until browned on both sides.

To Make Side Sauce:

Mix 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/2 cup water and 5 cloves of garlic.
