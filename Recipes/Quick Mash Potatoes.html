Quick Mash Potatoes

★★★★

Prep Time: 5 Cook Time: 15 Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2 Source: Blue Apron

Ingredients

10 ounces purple potatoes

2 tbsp Butter

Directions

Heat a small pot of salted water to boiling on high

Large dice the potatoes

Cut off and discard the root ends of the scallions; thinly slice the scallions, separating the white bottoms and green tops

Add the potatoes to the pot of boiling water.

Cook 10 to 12 minutes, or until tender when pierced with a fork.

Drain thoroughly and return to the pot. Off the heat, add the butter.

Using a fork, mash the cooked potatoes to your desired consistency.

Stir in the white bottoms of the scallions; season with salt and pepper to taste.

Cover and set aside in a warm place.
