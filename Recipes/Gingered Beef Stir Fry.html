Gingered Beef Stir Fry

★★★★

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 20 min Difficulty: Medium Servings: 4 serving

Ingredients

1 lb steak

2 tsp neutral oil

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp pepper

1 cup mushrooms

1 cup broccoli

scallions

1 pepper

3 tbsp ginger

3 tbsp cornstarch

3 tbsp soy sauce

3 tbsp water

1 cup rice

2 cup water

Directions

1. Slice pepper and scallions

2. Mince ginger

3. Cut steak into 1/8 inch slices

4. Combine ginger, cornstarch, soy sauce and water
