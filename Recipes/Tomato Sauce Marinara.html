Tomato Sauce Marinara

★★★★

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 1 hr 10 min Difficulty: Medium Servings: 4 serving

Ingredients

1 onion

8 garlic cloves

1 cup olive oil

3 can tomatoes

1/2 cup fresh basil

1 tsp salt

1 tsp pepper

1 tsp oregano

1/2 cup parsley

3 tbsp tomato paste

Directions

1. Chop onions

2. Mince garlic

3. Chop basil

4. Mash tomatoes

5. Sauté onions and garlic with olive oil 5 minutes until veggies are golden

6. Add everything else

7. Bring to boil

8. Lower heat and simmer for 1 hour stirring constantly
