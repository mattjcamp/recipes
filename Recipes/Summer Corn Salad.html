Summer Corn Salad

★★★

Prep Time: 10 Cook Time: 5 Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2 Source: Blue Apron

Ingredients

¼ Pound Mixed Cherry Tomatoes

2 Ears Corn

2 Ounces Arugula

1/2 Bunch Basil

1 Tablespoon Dijon Mustard

1 Tablespoon Red Wine Vinegar

Directions

Remove and discard the husks and silks of the corn. Cut the corn kernels off the cobs; discard the cobs.

Halve the tomatoes.

Pick the basil leaves off the stems; discard the stems.

In a medium pan, heat 2 teaspoons of olive oil on medium-high until hot. Add the corn; season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, 3 to 5 minutes, or until tender. Transfer to a large bowl.

Make the vinaigrette:
While the chicken cooks, in a small bowl, combine the vinegar and remaining mustard; season with salt and pepper to taste. Slowly whisk in 2 tablespoons of olive oil until thoroughly combined.

Make the salad & plate your dish:
To the bowl of cooked corn, add the arugula, tomatoes and the basil; season with salt and pepper. Add enough of the vinaigrette to coat the salad (you may have extra vinaigrette); toss to mix and season with salt and pepper to taste.
