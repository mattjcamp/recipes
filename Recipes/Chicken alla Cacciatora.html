Chicken alla Cacciatora

★★★★

Prep Time: 2 H 30 M Cook Time: 1 H 10 M Difficulty: Hard Servings: 3 servings Source: Foodnetwork.com

Ingredients

3 chicken breasts

2 cloves garlic

1 branch rosemary

2 large yellow onions

4 portobello mushrooms

4 ounces pancetta

1 cup white wine

1 can tomatoes

1 cup chicken stock

1 Pinch sugar

1 Pinch red chili flakes

Directions

PREP 1

1 Combine the garlic, rosemary, salt and pepper and enough olive oil to make a somewhat dry paste in a food processor. Process until the mixture is a paste.

2 Divide the paste evenly among the chicken and let sit, covered and refrigerated, for 2 hours.

PREP 2

1 Coarsely chop onions.

2 Cut portobello mushrooms into 1 inch cubes.

3 Process pancetta in the food processor to make a paste.

4 Purée tomatoes

COOK

1 In a Dutch oven, heat 3 tablespoons olive oil over high heat until smoking.

2 Brush the excess rub from the bird and sear each piece of meat on all sides in the hot oil.

3 Remove seared meat to a plate lined with paper towels.

4 Add the onions, the mushrooms and the pancetta and cook over high heat until onions are golden brown and fat has been rendered from pancetta.

4 Drain the excess oil, then add the wine and stir the bottom of the pan with a wooden spoon to dislodge the browned bits.

5 Add the tomato puree, chicken stock, sugar and chili flakes and bring to a boil.

6 Return the chicken to the pan. Cook for 20 minutes, covered.

7 Then cook 15 to 20 minutes more until sauce comes together, uncovered

8 Top with the sauce and serve immediately.
