Jiaozi Dumplings Mushik Style

★★★★

Difficulty: Hard Servings: 8 Source: Mom

Ingredients

1 lb ground beef or pork

4 scallions

4 garlic cloves

1/8 cup soy sauce

Wonton dough

Crisco or oil

Directions

Mix raw ground beef or pork, scallions, garlic and soy sauce in bowl.

Wrap 1 tbsp mix in won ton dough.

Cook in deep oil or crisco until browned on all sides.

To Make Side Sauce:

Mix 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/2 cup water and 5 cloves of garlic and one scallion.

Crisco to half wonton
Use heavy metal pan
Heat to 2.4 with 8 pieces
- make sure they just lightly fry
Cook five minutes each side
