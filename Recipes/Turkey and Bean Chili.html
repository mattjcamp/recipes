Turkey and Bean Chili

★★★★

Prep Time: 15m Cook Time: 40m Difficulty: Medium Servings: 4 Source: Realsimple.com

Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil

1 onion

1 green bell pepper

2 cloves garlic

1/2 lb ground turkey

2 tbsp tomato paste

1 tsp ground cumin

2 tsp chili powder

1 tbsp hot pepper flakes

Hot sauce

Guacamole

1 can tomatoes (28-ounce)

2 cans 15-ounce) kidney beans

Directions

NOTES

Try the Brian L method of low and slow oven cook instead of stove top https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8h7YSpu98Uc

1 Chop garlic, onion and bell pepper.

2 Rinse beans.

3 Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.

4 Add the onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally, until tender, 4 to 6 minutes.

5 Add the turkey and cook, breaking up with a spoon, until no longer pink, 3 to 5 minutes.

6 Stir in the tomato paste, cumin, and chili pepper. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute.

7 Add the tomatoes and their juices, beans, ½ cup water, 1 ¼ teaspoons salt, and ¼ teaspoon pepper. Bring to a boil.

8 Reduce heat and simmer, add chili flakes and hot sauce, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened, 12 to 15 minutes.

Notes

Careful that one package of ground meat is usually one pound (16 ounces) so you should double the recipe or freeze the other half. Otherwise it gets dry.
