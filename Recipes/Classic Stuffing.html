Classic Stuffing

★★★

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 40 min Difficulty: Medium Servings: 8 Source: Mom

Ingredients

1 loaf of white bread

2 celery

1 onion

1 carrot

2 cup stock

1 egg

1 half stick butter

1 tsp. poultry seasoning

1 tsp sage

1/2 tsp salt

1/4 tsp pepper

Directions

Notes try taller pan go get less crispy
Dont over do the poultry seasoning lol

1.) Cut bread in cubes and let sit overnight uncovered to get stale.
(Don't use the pre-cut stuffing bread in the packages).

2.) Chop celery, onion and carrot

3.) Simmer celery, carrot and onion in melted butter.

4.) Let cool a little.

5.) In large bowl mix together cooked celery, onion, carrot and cubes of bread, poultry seasoning, sage, salt and pepper.

6.) Beat egg and add to chicken broth and then add to bread mixture. (use a little more chicken broth if not moist enough).

7.) Rinse and pat dry (with paper towels) cavity of bird. Stuff chicken or turkey. (Or bake 40 minutes 375 degrees)
