BLT Sandwhich Bread

★★★★★

Prep Time: 4 hr Cook Time: 45 min Difficulty: Medium Source: Brian Lagerstrom

Ingredients

350g or 1.5 cup warm water

3g or 1 tsp instant yeast

530g or 4 1/4 cup Bread Flour

12g or 1 tbsp sugar

20g or 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil

125g or 2/3 cup ripe poolish or sourdough starter

11g or 2 tsp salt

Directions

Combine everything in a bowl. Squeeze and pinch with a wet hand or dough whisk.

Flip into a bowl and allow to rise at room temp for 2 hours doing slap and folds every 30 minutes for a total of three times

Prepare 1.5lb/10"x5.5"x3" loaf pan by liberally oiling bottom and sides with olive oil.

Flip dough onto a floured work surface

De-gas with fingertips

Shape loaf as shown @8:04

Transfer to prepared loaf pan seam side down, cover, and allow to rise at room temp for 1 hour

Diagonally score 4-5 times and bake in preheated 425F/218C oven for 40-45min.
