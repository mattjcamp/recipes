Pumpkin Pie

★★★★★

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 1 hr Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 servings Source: My Collection

Ingredients

2 eggs

1/2 cup sugar

1 tsp ground cinnamon

1 tsp nutmeg

1 tsp ginger

1/2 tsp cloves

1 can pumpkin

1 1/4 cups cream

Directions

Preheat oven to 375°F.

Beat the eggs with the sugar.

Then add the spices and a pinch of salt.

Stir in the pumpkin purée and then the creme.

Pour the pumpkin mixture into the still‐hot crust and bake for 60 minutes, until the mixture shakes like Jell‐O but is still quite moist.

Cool on a rack and serve warm or at room temperature.

Notes

Need 1.5 times when using basic pie crust.

Reduce spices a little

The best ways to finish this pie: Top with Whipped Cream flavored with cinnamon, nutmeg, or ginger if you like.
