Dutch Oven Barbacoa

★★★★★

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 4 hours Total Time: 4.5 hours Difficulty: Easy Source: simpleandfraiche.com

Ingredients

3 lbs Beef Chuck Roast

1 Onion (sliced)

1 1/2 Tbsp Chili Powder

1/2 tsp Cumin

4 cloves Garlic (chopped)

3 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar

1.5 cups Beef Broth

3 Bay leaves (dried)

1 Lime (to squeeze on top after cooked)

Directions

1. Preheat a large dutch oven over medium-high heat. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees.
2. Pat the Chuck Roast dry and season with sea salt and pepper. Drizzle the dutch oven with Olive Oil and sear the beef for 3-4 minute per side, or until browned.
3. Remove the beef from the dutch oven, and reduce the heat to low.
4. Add the onion along with a pinch of salt and saute for about 5 minutes or until the onions have softened.
5. Add the garlic, cumin and chili powder and cook for thirty seconds or until fragrant.
6. Add the broth and apple cider vinegar, and bring to a boil. Taste for seasonings, and add 1/4-1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper, if needed.
7. Reduce the heat, and add the beef back into the dutch oven along with the bay leaves. Put the lid on the dutch oven and place in the oven for 3.5-4 hours.
8. Once the meat is fork tender, remove it from the dutch oven and shred.
9. Pour the liquid from the dutch oven into your blender and blend on high speed until smooth.
10. Place the shredded beef and blended liquid back in the dutch oven and combine. Top with a squeeze of fresh lime juice.
