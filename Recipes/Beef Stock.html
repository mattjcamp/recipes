Beef Stock

★★★★★

Synced

Prep Time: 1 hour Cook Time: 16 hours Total Time: 16 hours Difficulty: Medium Source: @mattjcamp

Ingredients

Stock

3 1⁄2 lb beef bones, oxtail, short rib, knuckle, and shank 1 Tbsp vinegar

Flavoring Ideas

2 celery, cut and roasted

1 onion, cut and roasted

2 carrot, cut and roasted

1 whole head garlic, halved crosswise, cut and roasted

2 bay leaves, or more to taste

toasted spices, peppercorns, parsley, herbs, whole spices

asian flavorings, optional: use Pho or other Asian seasonings depending on recipe

Directions

Blanch Bones
1. Boil the bones for 5 minutes and then rinse them off and clean the pot well Roast Bones
2. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Place beef bones on the prepared baking sheet.
4. Roast bones in the pre-heated oven for 40 minutes, turning over at the halfway point.
Simmer
5. Cover the bones with water in a dutch oven
6. Pre-heat oven to 190 degrees F.
7. Place dutch oven in oven overnight and check in the first two hours occasionally to skim
off any foam or gunk that rises.
8. Add flavoring the next day for the last hour of cooking. Carrots, celery, onions with peels, toasted spices, peppercorns, herbs.

Notes

This beef bone broth recipe makes a nourishing soup or homemade base for stews and can be frozen for later use but should be consumed within 6 months.
