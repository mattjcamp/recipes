Heirloom Tomato Salad with Basil-Yogurt Drizzle

★★★

Cook Time: 10 min Servings: Serves 4 Source: Wholefoodsmarket.com

Ingredients

1 teaspoon coriander seeds

2 pounds heirloom tomatoes, sliced if large or medium, halved or quartered if very small

1/2 cup plain yogurt (full-fat or low-fat)

1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, finely chopped

1/2 shallot, finely chopped

1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt

Directions

Toast coriander seeds in a small dry pan, moving constantly, until fragrant, about 2 minutes.

Let toasted seeds cool on the counter or a cutting board.

Then crush seeds with the base of a small pan or a large serving spoon, set aside.

Fan tomatoes on a platter, overlapping as needed.

Stir yogurt, basil, shallot and salt together; drizzle over tomatoes.

Sprinkle with coriander and serve immediately.

Nutrition

Per serving: 60 calories (15 from fat), 1.5g total fat, 0.5g saturated fat, 5mg cholesterol, 320mg sodium, 11g carbohydrates (3g dietary fiber, 8g sugar), 3g protein.
